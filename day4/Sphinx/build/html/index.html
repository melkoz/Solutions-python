simple_math.poly_first(x, a0, a1)¶

First degree polynomial where a0 is the y-intercept and a1 is the slope.

Parameters
x, a0, a1integer or float.
Returns
a0 + a1*xinteger or float.

Examples

>>> poly_first(2,2,2)  
 6
>>> poly_first(2,2,2.5)
 7.0
